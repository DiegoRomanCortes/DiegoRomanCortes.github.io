Research & Physics

Photonic Lattices with Orbital Modes

Master's thesis on multi-orbital photonic lattices using femtosecond laser writing. Studied mode coupling, symmetry breaking, and analogies with molecular orbitals and topological systems.

Keywords: photonics, symmetry, quantum simulation

Initial Condition Engineering in Optical Lattices

Designed and implemented a flexible optical setup to excite custom initial conditions in femtosecond-written lattices, allowing experimental studies of wavepacket dynamics and localization.

Keywords: experimental optics, lattice dynamics

Software & Tools

Custom Eigenvalue Solver for Coupled Mode Theory

Wrote a modular eigensolver from scratch in Python/C to study wave dynamics in coupled waveguide systems. Includes support for non-Hermitian matrices, orbital degrees of freedom, and sparse structures.

Tech: Python, NumPy, SciPy, C

Blog Engine in Pure JS (Coming soon)

Building a lightweight blog engine for this site with native Markdown parsing, tag filters, and no external dependencies. Fully open and hackable.

Tech: HTML, JS, marked.js
